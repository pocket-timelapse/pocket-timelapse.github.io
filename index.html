Supplemental Videos

This gallery shows some of the results generated by the Pocket Time-Lapse system. We present time-lapse videos generated using both a linear interpolation as well as our new Time Splatting method. Contents of the page are as follows:

  • Time Splatting results on Pocket-Timelapse datasets: Seasonal and time-of-day progressions reconstructed with Time Splatting.
  • Linear interpolation on Pocket-Timelapse datasets:
    • Seasonal and time-of-day progressions generated with linear interpolation.
  • Time Splatting results on traditional timelapses:
    • Time Splatting can aid in time-lapse reconstruction of not only Pocket-Timelapses, but also traditional time-lapses taken with webcams.
  • Composite Image Results
  • Intrinsic Image Decomposition comparisons
